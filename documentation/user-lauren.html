Persona: Lauren Douglass

User Story

As a large dog owner I would like to know where my dog can have the most open space to run around when we play fetch, as well as knowing the best places for her to socialize with other dogs.

Technology

  • iPhone 13 pro
  • Series 7 apple watch
  • Macbook Air running iOS Monterey

Proficiency

  • Main apps: Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, Twitter and TikTok.
  • Use Excel spreadsheet for block art design and Adobe Illustrator as a hobby.
  • Shares her artwork on all of her social media apps.
  • Also proficient with using excel for scheduling.

Attitudes and Needs

  • Loves to take her dog for a walk after work.
  • Needs more open space for her large dog to stretch its legs.
  • Recently moved to ABQ and is unfamiliar with the area.
  • Needs a place for her dog to socialize with other dogs.

Frustrations

  • Apartment complex she lives in only has a small dog area for play and potty-time.
  • The dog park near her apartment is rundown and doesn't like to be out too late there after work.
  • Great Danes are excellent apartment dogs but her dog still needs to socialize.

Pre User Experience

User is unfamiliar with the Area she lives in and has a hard time getting around. User wishes to find a reputable dog park that is big enough for her dog to play and socialize with other dogs. A work friend of Lauren recommended "The Best friend" app to find specific parks that have reviews and also has a community page that help her find other dogs to play and socialize with.

Interaction Flow

  • User downloads "The Best friend" app
  • The first page the User lands on is the Welcome/home page. User is then prompted to create a new profile.
  • User clicks on "create profile" on top-right middle section of page.
  • User is moved to the create profile page. User then fills out fields, uploads photo of her dog. When finished filling correct info User taps "submit".
  • After being to new profile page User taps "Home".
  • User returns home, on the page user taps the ABQ Dog parks links to look up dog parks and reviews for bigger park spaces.
  • After searching for a few minutes User finds park that looked suitable for her large dog.
  • User puts directions into travel google maps.
  • Closes app and drives toward park with her dog.

Post User Experience

With Dog Park app user was able to find a park that was highly recommend by other user in the community. Belle was able to play and socialize with other dogs. Furthermore, User really looks forward to using this app in the future park visits Belle enjoys the open spaces and making friends with other dogs. User was also able to upload photos of her dog at the park for the other dog park community app users to see, like and comment under the photo.
