Persona: Sara Jones

User Story

As a dog owner, I would like to socialize online with other dog owners in my community.

Technology

  • iPhone 14 running iOS 16
  • Apple watch
  • iMac desktop running iOS Monterey

Proficiency

  • Loves the convenience of technology but is not glued to it and finds time to disconnect.
  • Main apps: Instagram, Facebook, Yelp and Amazon.
  • Uses a computer all day at work, mostly Microsoft Office and Google Documents.
  • Facebook is her favorite app to communicate with family & friends.

Attitudes and Needs

  • Loves her dog and would like to have more doggy play dates
  • Wants to be sure she takes her dog to a safe/clean park
  • Would like to be able to ask for animal care recommendations or setup play-dates with other owners online.

Frustrations

  • Very busy with work so any free time must be planned out in advanced
  • Last time Sara went to a dog park there were no other dogs for Furball to play with
  • Likes to use Yelp but wishes there was more of a community feeling to interact with other dog owners

Interaction Flow

  • I went to the Dog Park Community site and clicked on "create a pet profile"
  • The site took me to a page where I provided information needed.
  • I used the input form to provide my email address, created a password, my dog's name, uploaded a photo of Furball, and clicked "submit"
  • The site then took me to my profile page where I can see Furball's profile photo, I see a section where I can create photo posts, I can also see my default dog park which I selected while registering, and below that are two other buttons, one for "my friends" and "make new friends"
  • I clicked on "make new friends"
  • The site took me to a page where I am able to view other doggy profiles, I followed a few cute dogs profiles and I am now able to comment and chat with other owners in the area.

Post User Experience

With Dog Park app I was able to find a way to interact with local dog owners and share doggy care ideas as well as schedule play dates for Furball.
